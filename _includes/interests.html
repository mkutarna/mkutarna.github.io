Interests

Personal Interests

  • Making things. A broad statement; I am often designing and putting together new ideas - 3D printing, prototyping, hand tool woodworking, etc. I recently attended the International Symposium on Academic Makerspaces 2019 @ Yale.
  • Amateur radio. I've been interested in electronics and communication since I was young - but only as an adult am I diving into the world of amateur radio communication. I am studying for my advanced certification, in order to begin designing and building my own equipment.
  • Hiking. As a lifeline hiker, living in the Lower Mainland has provided many opportunities to get out into the wilderness. I enjoy taking my family to explore what nature has hidden away in the area.

Recent Projects

  • Electronics schedule display. A display for meeting rooms and teaching labs to replace paper schedules. Based on a RPi3, this display fetches data from a meeting room booking system to show up-to-date information at the door.
  • Custom VHF/UHF receiver. I'm designing, building and testing a VHF/UHF receiver to expand my skills in RF design (using Altium) and assembly (printing PCB via Voltera printer). This will be one building block a larger APRS-equipped transceiver project.
  • Pikler triangle. Hand built a folding, pine-and-maple Pikler triangle for my children to enjoy inside during the wet, grey months of Vancouver-area winter.
